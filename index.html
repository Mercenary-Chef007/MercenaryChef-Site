About Chef Christian Briner

With over 30 years in the hospitality industry and 15 years as a freelance chef, Christian Briner crafts extraordinary culinary experiences in some of the world’s most remote and rugged environments...

Christian’s expertise also extends to the Caribbean and Pacific Coast, where he’s served as private yacht chef for elite clientele. A life lived abroad—in Brazil, Peru, Central America, Hawaii, and Alaska—has shaped his craft with a holistic approach rooted in cultural depth and natural connection. Raised in his grandmother’s catering kitchen, he forged a tireless work ethic and a reverence for hospitality that continues to define his culinary journey.
